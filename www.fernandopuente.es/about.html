I'm an art director, designer & photographer based in Madrid.
I currently work as creative & design director at EL GRITO & EC Brands Studio at El Confidencial.

With over 12 years of experience as a designer, I've been focused on digital design, collaborating with some of the most recognizable brands, including Tous, Mazda, Netflix, Amazon, Volkswagen, and many others.
As photographer, I have worked with editorial, fashion, and lifestyle brands.

I am also the founder of MIRAMONO magazine and a member of the design jury panel at @Awwwards.
